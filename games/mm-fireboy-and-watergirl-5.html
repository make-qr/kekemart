About this game

Play 5 - Elements free online — a puzzle browser game on MonkeyMart.one. No download, no install. Works on desktop and mobile.

How to play

Tap objects on the board.
Collect three identical items.
Matching triples disappear automatically.
Clear the entire board to complete the level.
Use hints and boosters when needed.
Progress through levels and discover more objects.
