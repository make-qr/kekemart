Frequently Asked Questions

What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is an online gaming platform that offers a variety of free-to-play games in your web browser.

Is it free to play games on Monkey Mart?

Yes, all games on Monkey Mart are free to play. Some games may offer optional in-game purchases.

Do I need to create an account to play?

While most games can be played without an account, creating one allows you to save your progress and participate in the community.

How do I report a technical issue?

You can report technical issues through our Contact page or by emailing support@monkeymart.one.

Are the games suitable for children?

Most games are family-friendly, but we recommend parents review the content ratings provided for each game.
